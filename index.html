Discord to Blog - Local Development

This is a temporary local development page. To properly view your AEM site, you need to register it with Adobe Edge Delivery Services.

To register your site:

  1. Go to https://www.hlx.live/
  2. Sign in with your Adobe ID
  3. Click "Add Site" and connect your GitHub repository (rwunsch/EDS-Hackatron-Discord)
  4. Complete the onboarding process

Once registered, your site will be available at: https://main--EDS-Hackatron-Discord--rwunsch.hlx.page

Project Files:

  • index.md - Homepage content
  • blog.md - Blog index page
  • generate-posts.js - Discord to blog generator
